How to Use

Type in or paste in level names separated by new lines. Sometimes, the generator will output a {CR} or a {LF}, which you need to replace manually because copy and paste will just turn those into a whole different character.

In Notepad++, the characters can be replaced like this:

(Replace {CR} with \r and {LF} with \n, both using Extended mode)


Generator

Type your level names into the generator (Each line is a seperate name).


How to rename levels

First, find your level with CTRL+F, make sure its only your level and not a text containing your level.
Then, replace the level name with and character infront of it with the output from the generator.

Enjoy!
